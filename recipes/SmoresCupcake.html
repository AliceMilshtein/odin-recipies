Smore's Cupcake

This is the recipe for a smore's cupcake. It is a chocolate cupcake with a graham cracker base and topped with a marshamllow frosting.

Ingredients

  • chocolate cupcake
  • graham crackers
  • butter
  • sugar
  • marshmallow frosting

Directions

  1. Add mixture of graham crackers,sugar, butter to cupcake liners. Toast for 6 min
  2. Add cupcake on top
  3. Frost with frosting
  4. Toast frosting under broiler
